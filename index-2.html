About Conference

The 3rd International Conference on Frontiers in Computing and Systems (intCSD-2023) will be held during December 19-21, 2022, at Indian Institute of Technology Ropar, Punjab, India, to be organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering. The main objective of intCSD-2023 is going to present the latest research and results of scientists with a theme “Cyber- physical Systems for real-life applications” pertaining to to AI, Machine Learning & Data Science; Devices, Circuits and Systems; Computational Biology, Biomedical Informatics and Network Medicine; Communication Networks, Cloud computing and IoT; Image, Video and Signal Processing; Security and Privacy. This conference provides opportunities for the delegates from different areas to exchange new ideas and application experiences face to face, to establish academic or research collaborations. It will bring together the researchers from academia and industry onto a common platform and help them to develop a comprehensive understanding of the challenges of technological advancements from different viewpoints. This conference also will help in fostering a healthy and vibrant relationship between academia and industry.
