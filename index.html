About Conference

The 1st International Conference on Intelligent Computing for Sustainable Development (intCSD-2022) will be held on 9 - 10 feb 2023 at Chandigarh College of Engineering and Technology (Degree wing), Panjab University to be organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering. The main objective of intCSD-2022 is to present the latest research and results of scientists with a theme “Intelligent Computing for Sustainable Development”. This conference provides opportunities for the delegates from different areas to exchange new ideas and application experiences face to face, to establish academic or research collaborations. It will bring together the researchers from academia and industry onto a common platform and help them to develop a comprehensive understanding of the challenges of technological advancements from different viewpoints. This conference also will help in fostering a healthy and vibrant relationship between academia and industry.


This conference offers a unique international platform for a wide range of professions including scholar's researcher, academician, industry professionals and other technology enthusiastic peoples to discuss, present and exchange their knowledge and experience to give more reliable and efficient solutions with the help of intelligent computing for sustainable development. It also helps to explore the various intelligent computing technologies which leads to minimizing human effort and cost by giving the sustainable development in different fields. This conference invites researchers to contribute original research articles, innovative ideas, theory, methodologies. Reviews and other emerging designs/proposals that will seek the continuing efforts to understand the novel techniques that lead to future improvements and Recent advancements in Intelligent Computing for Sustainable Development.
